The Challenge: Fragmented Logistics

In Uganda and much of Sub-Saharan Africa, the logistics sector is plagued by **inefficiency, opacity, and fragmentation**, particularly for those who need it most:

  • High Costs & Unreliability:** Rural farmers, women-led construction sites, traders, and NGOs struggle to find affordable, trustworthy transport for their goods, often relying on informal, costly brokers.
  • **Zero Visibility:** Manual methods mean no live tracking, causing anxiety and miscommunication about goods in transit.
  • **Underutilized Capacity:** Thousands of informal truck and van owners lack consistent jobs and digital tools to optimize their routes and income.
  • **No Proof of Delivery:** Verification is manual, unreliable, or entirely missing, leading to disputes and losses.

This results in significant losses, with rural SMEs losing up to **25% of perishable inventory** due to disorganized delivery.

Our Solution: FikaConnect

FikaConnect is a transformative digital platform that **bridges the gap**, connecting senders directly with a verified network of transporters. We provide:

  • **Seamless Booking:** A mobile-responsive platform to easily request pickups, choose vehicle types (trucks, vans, boda-bodas), and specify cargo.
  • **Real-time GPS Tracking:** Monitor your goods live via Google Maps API, from pickup to drop-off.
  • **Digital Proof-of-Delivery:** Transporters upload photo proof, and senders receive instant confirmation with PDF receipts.
  • **Smart Dashboards:** Dedicated, intuitive interfaces for senders, transporters, and dispatchers for full operational control.
  • **Instant Communication:** Receive updates via SMS, WhatsApp, or in-app notifications.

Every delivery is now **traceable, trusted, and visible**, optimizing operations and empowering local businesses.
